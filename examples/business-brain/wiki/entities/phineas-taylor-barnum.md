# P. T. Barnum

Phineas Taylor Barnum (1810-1891), American showman and businessman, founder of
what became the Barnum & Bailey circus. He made, lost, and rebuilt a fortune, and
distilled the experience into the 1880 lecture [[art-of-money-getting]].

Relevance to this brain:

- Source of the practical rules: [[economy-and-avoiding-debt]], focus over
  scattering, and his share of [[perseverance]].
- Writes from hands-on commerce, a useful counterweight to Wattles' abstraction
  (see [[mindset-vs-method]]).
