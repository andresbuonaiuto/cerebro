# Wallace D. Wattles

American writer (1860-1911) associated with the New Thought movement. Best known
for [[science-of-getting-rich]] (1910), which frames wealth as the result of a
repeatable method rather than luck.

Relevance to this brain:

- Source of the [[creative-vs-competitive-mind]] distinction and
  [[acting-in-the-present]].
- His register is metaphysical; this brain keeps the behavioral core and flags
  the metaphysics as period framing (see the source note's limitations).
