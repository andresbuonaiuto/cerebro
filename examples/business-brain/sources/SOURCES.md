# Sources

Provenance of the original documents this brain was built from. Full texts are
not committed here; both are public domain and freely available at the links
below. In a real brain, `sources/` holds the originals (and `sources/converted/`
their markdown); this example keeps only the provenance to stay lightweight.

| Slug | Work | Author | Year | Status | Where to read |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|

Both works are in the public domain in the United States because they were
published before 1929. They are used here purely to demonstrate the cerebro
pattern on real, freely reusable material.
